What is BPO?

BPO stands for Business Process Outsourcing, which involves contracting specific business operations to a third-party service provider to improve efficiency and reduce costs.

Business Process Outsourcing is a strategic practice where companies delegate non-core business functions to external specialized providers. This comprehensive approach includes customer service, technical support, data entry, human resources, accounting, and various operational tasks. BPO enables organizations to leverage expert knowledge, advanced technologies, and economies of scale while maintaining focus on their primary business objectives.
